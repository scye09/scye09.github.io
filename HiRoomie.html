HiRoomie is a mobile app designed for college students that facilitates communication & collaboration between roommates.



WHAT IS IT?


Creating HiRoomie was one of my first experiences working with UI/UX design. It is the product of a 6-week design seminar and the frustrations that most college students feel at some point when living with someone else.

Roommate issues are a common problem both on and off college campuses. These issues include:

  • Roommates not delegating household tasks (e.g. washing dishes, taking out trash, buying toilet paper, etc.)
  • Roommates delegating tasks but feeling too awkward to remind one another when one roommate forgets to complete an agreed-upon task
  • Roommates repeating tasks because there is lack of awareness that the task has already been completed by someone else (e.g. buying more toilet paper even though it has already been replenished)

It seemed that the root of most issues was often poor communication, so solving this problem became the focus of our application.



PROCESS




WHAT'S IN THE FINAL APP?


Multi-Group Feature: Users can switch, create, and join multiple groups at a time.

We realized in our user research that students may live with different people in different residences throughout the year - for example, I lived in a 4-person suite during the school year but lived in a 2-person apartment in San Francisco during my internship.

Task List Feature: Users can view, add, delete, and send a reminder about roommate tasks and upcoming events.

The remind feature was inspired by the same feature in Venmo, which helps reduce some of the awkwardness associated around reminding friends to do something.

Grocery List Feature: Users can view, add, and check off grocery list items on a shared list.



Future Improvements


  • Roommate Listing Feature: Add a feature that allows users to find other people that are also seeking a roommate in their location.
  • Chat Feature: Create a chatbox for users to use to communicate with one another.
  • Calendar Integration Feature: Add a feature that allows users to import schedules or events from other calendar apps in order to facilitate scheduling process.
